**Security Operations Center Incident Report**

**Incident Summary:**
A potential brute force attack was detected from IP address 192.168.1.45, with multiple failed login attempts recorded. The incident was identified by the Log Agent and confirmed by the Threat Agent.

**Threat Level:**
⚠️ High (🟠)

**Malware Analysis:**
No malware indicators were found by the Malware Agent, suggesting that the attack is likely focused on credential exploitation rather than malware deployment.

**Response Recommendation:**
To mitigate the threat, it is recommended to:
1. Block the suspicious IP address (192.168.1.45) to prevent further login attempts.
2. Lock the affected user account to prevent potential unauthorized access.
3. Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) to enhance account security.
4. Monitor login attempts for the next 24 hours to detect and respond to potential follow-up attacks.

**Final Decision:**
Based on the analysis and recommendations, it is decided to implement the proposed response measures to ensure the security of our systems and prevent potential breaches. The incident will be continuously monitored, and additional actions will be taken as necessary.
